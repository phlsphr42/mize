User Guide

Welcome to Mize

Mize is a Magic: The Gathering match tracking and analysis platform built for competitive players. Log your matches, analyze your opening hands, and obtain insights into what the data says about the metagame.

Entering Game Data

At the start of each game, log your opening hand. Once the game is over, select your opponent's archetype, and record whether you won or lost. The whole process takes less than a minute per match.

Every match you log contributes to your personal performance history and the community dataset that powers Mize's analysis features.

Opening Hand Analysis

Tells you which cards in your deck are making a difference in your win rate.

Bayesian Analysis

Compares your win rate in games where a card appeared in your opening hand versus games where it didn't. A Bayesian adjustment accounts for small sample sizes so cards with few appearances are appropriately weighted.

Chi-Squared Analysis

Applies a statistical significance test to tell you how confident you can be that a win rate difference is real and not just variance.

**Highly significant (p < 0.01) — very unlikely to be a coincidence
*Significant (p < 0.05) — probably a real effect
nsNot significant — not enough evidence yet
⚠Insufficient data — sample size too small for meaningful analysis, but trend is shown
Meta Matchup Matrix

A grid showing how every archetype performs against every other archetype. Green cells indicate a favorable matchup, red cells indicate an unfavorable one. Cells with fewer than 5 games show "—" to indicate insufficient data.

Tap any cell to see exact game counts and win rates for that matchup. Use this to identify which matchups your deck struggles with and make informed sideboard decisions.

MTGO Deck Rankings

Tracks how every archetype is performing in competitive MTGO events, sourced daily from published decklists and standings.

Opportunity Score

Rewards decks outperforming their metagame share. Underplayed strong decks rank highest, useful for finding decks that not everyone has caught on to yet.

Raw Performance

Rewards high win rate combined with large sample size. Dominant popular decks rank highest, useful for identifying what is objectively performing best in the current metagame and what you should expect to face at competitive events.

Your Data

Your personal match history belongs to you. Individual game records are only visible in your personal analysis views and are never displayed to other users by name. Community analysis features use aggregated data across all pilots without identifying individuals.
